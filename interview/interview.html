Interview Preparation

1. Resume & Profile

  • Keep resume concise (1-2 pages). Highlight key projects and impact.
  • Maintain an updated LinkedIn and GitHub with sample projects.

2. Technical Interviews

Focus areas:

  • Data Structures & Algorithms (coding problems)
  • System design for senior roles
  • Language-specific knowledge (e.g., Java, JS)

3. Behavioral Interviews

  • Prepare STAR-format answers for common questions
  • Discuss challenges, leadership, and collaboration examples

4. Coding Interview Tips

  • Clarify requirements and ask questions first
  • Discuss approach & complexity before coding
  • Test edge cases and walk through your solution

5. System Design Basics

For larger roles, practice designing scalable systems, trade-offs, and CAP/consistency issues. Sketch components, databases, and APIs.

6. Resources

  • LeetCode
  • Pramp (mock interviews)
  • System Design Primer
